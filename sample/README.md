# Competitive App UX Research

Deep-dive visual UX/design research on habit, wellness, focus, journaling, astrology, tarot, witchy-routine, and related apps + games. Each report gathers screenshots and embeds them inline with captions, then ends with a `Design language & takeaways` section.

**Total: 38 reports.** See [../apps-index.md](../apps-index.md) for the full catalog with ✅/⬜/📝/⏭ status markers.

## Tend-specific framing

Tend is a daily routine app with a witchy/pagan/occult aesthetic where habits are "offerings" to patron deities. Every report's takeaway section is written through that lens.

## Cross-cutting themes for Tend

**Direct mechanical parallels** — Cult of the Lamb (sermons/rituals/doctrine/followers), Cosmic Wheel Sisterhood (user-built ritual artifacts), Inscryption (card sacrifice as offering), Hades (god-as-blessing-provider), Spiritfarer ("tend" verb literally), SuperBetter (real-life-as-game reframing).

**Aesthetic candidates** (pick a lane — each is internally consistent; mixing breaks the brand):
- **Warm-witchy editorial** — CHANI (oxblood/midnight + magazine type)
- **Folk-pagan AAA** — REKA (amber/moss/oak Slavic)
- **Storybook woodcut** — Wytchwood (medieval-fantasy hand-drawn)
- **Victorian botanical occult** — Strange Horticulture
- **Candlelit folk horror** — Inscryption (90% darkness, 10% warm light)
- **Modern mythology** — Hades 2 (witchy-green Art Deco)
- **Vaporwave-neon** — Mystic Mondays (queer Instagram-native)
- **Mignola ink** — Darkest Dungeon (oppressive monochrome with regional accents)

**Deity-portrait references** — Hades (40+ Olympian portraits, signature-color-per-god), Darkest Dungeon (Mignola hero classes incl. Occultist), Cult of the Lamb (four bishop bosses), Labyrinthos (minimalist line-art system).

**Correspondence library models** — Strange Horticulture (botanical book pages), Habitica (pet/mount collection), Hades (lore codex), Labyrinthos (tarot library + lessons).

**Loss aversion + mercy mechanics** — SleepTown (visible damage in success-spot), Forest (withered trees), Habitica (HP + party damage), Duolingo (Streak Freeze). Tend version suggested: "Patron's Forgiveness" earnable via devotion, giftable from coven members.

**Notification voice** — Co-Star (brutal parasocial), Finch (anthropomorphic softness), Duolingo (guilt-tripping owl), Stoic (philosophical), CHANI (warm-essayistic). "Brigid is disappointed" inherits the witchy frame and avoids the corporate-manipulation problem.

**Anti-patterns to avoid** — Moonly's paywall pressure (fake countdowns, hidden skip), skeuomorphic grimoire apps (literal 3D candles, parchment textures over typographic hierarchy), midnight-blue-and-gold as a generic category default.
