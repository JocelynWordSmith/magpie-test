## Habit & Routine Trackers (RPG-style)

- ✅ **Habitica** ([report](../competitive-apps/habitica.md)) — Gamified habit tracker with deep RPG mechanics: classes, parties, quests, pets, and HP damage from missed dailies that hurts your whole party. The benchmark for "habits as a full RPG."
- ✅ **EpicWin** ([report](../competitive-apps/epicwin.md)) — One-time-purchase RPG to-do list where completing tasks triggers attack animations against your task entries, earning XP and loot for your chosen avatar.
- ⬜ **Level Up Life** — Subscription RPG built around six core attributes (Energy, Discipline, Focus, Confidence, Creativity, Connection); tasks earn XP that levels up specific life skills.
- ✅ **LifeUp** ([report](../competitive-apps/lifeup.md)) — Open-source Android habit tracker with a fully user-defined economy: design your own custom skill attributes, XP values, and reward shop with self-set prices.

## Habit & Routine Trackers (minimalist)

- ✅ **Streaks** ([report](../competitive-apps/streaks.md)) — Apple Design Award–winning iOS-only habit tracker with a deliberate 24-task cap and ring-based completion UI. Deep HealthKit integration auto-completes health habits.
- ⬜ **Productive** — Clean iOS-native habit tracker with morning/afternoon/evening time-slot scheduling, light gamification, and global community challenges.
- ⬜ **Bearable** — Comprehensive health/mood/symptom tracker focused on correlation insights for chronic illness management. Included as a "zero gamification" contrast case.

## Todo & Productivity Apps (well-designed)

Adjacent to Tend's space — these are studied for craft, information architecture, and design polish rather than gamification or witchy aesthetics. They set the "Linear-tier polish" ceiling Tend should aspire to.

- ✅ **Things 3** ([report](sample/things-3.md)) — Cultured Code's Apple Design Award-winning task manager. The gold standard for minimal, refined, considered iOS app design. Famous for the "Today" / "Upcoming" / "Anytime" / "Someday" structure and a magazine-quality typographic hierarchy.
- ✅ **Todoist** ([report](sample/todoist.md)) — Cross-platform task manager with natural-language input ("buy milk tomorrow at 5pm"). Clean modern design, well-considered projects/labels/filters/karma system, generous free tier.
- ⬜ **TickTick** — All-in-one productivity: tasks, habits, pomodoro timer, calendar, sleep tracking. Strong feature-density-without-clutter execution and the only major todo with built-in habit tracking.
- ⬜ **Sunsama** — Calm, intentional daily planner that pulls in tasks from email/Slack/Notion/Asana and forces you to plan a realistic day. Beautiful warm design, anti-overwhelm philosophy. Closest spiritually to a "daily routine" app.
- ✅ **Linear** ([report](sample/linear.md)) — Software project management. The reference for "Linear-tier polish" — keyboard-first, instant UI response, opinionated information architecture, exemplary use of color and typography in a dense product surface.
- ⬜ **Notion Calendar** (formerly Cron) — Calendar app with high design polish: multi-account, native menu-bar, keyboard shortcuts, instant rendering. Acquired by Notion in 2024.
- ⬜ **Sorted 3** — Time-blocking task manager with a beautiful day-grid schedule visualization. "Hyper-scheduling" mode auto-schedules undone tasks. iOS/Mac only.
- ⬜ **Akiflow** — Daily planning hub combining tasks, calendar, and email triage into one keyboard-driven surface. Strong information architecture for a complex problem space.
- ⬜ **Routinery** — ADHD-friendly sequential routine builder. Tap "start morning routine" and it walks you through each step with a timer. Closest existing UX to Tend's "ritual sequence" framing.
- ⬜ **OmniFocus** — The power-user GTD reference. Less visually exciting than Things, but exemplary information architecture for complex task hierarchies, defer/due distinction, and perspectives (saved filtered views).

## Self-Care & Companion Pet Apps

- ✅ **Finch** ([report](../competitive-apps/finch.md)) — Self-care app where you raise a virtual baby bird whose energy comes from your completed goals and reflections; the bird then "goes on adventures" and returns with reflective questions.
- ✅ **Focus Friend** ([report](../competitive-apps/focus-friend.md)) — Hank Green's cozy focus timer where a cute "Bean" knits socks and scarves while you work; leaving the app makes the Bean drop its needles.

## Focus / Pomodoro Timers

- ✅ **Forest** ([report](../competitive-apps/forest.md)) — Plant a virtual tree at the start of a focus session; the tree dies if you leave the app, and the forest of survived trees becomes a visual log of your productive time. Partners with Trees for the Future to plant real trees.
- ⬜ **Flora** — Forest-like focus timer with multi-user group planting sessions for study-group accountability. iOS-only, requires Facebook login.
- ⬜ **Plantie** — Pomodoro timer with a four-stage plant growth cycle (seed → sprout → young → mature) and 15+ collectible species. iOS-focused.
- ✅ **Virtual Cottage** ([report](../competitive-apps/virtual-cottage.md)) — Ambient productivity environment on Steam: sit in a cozy pixel-art cottage with lo-fi music and rain sounds while a focus timer runs. The atmosphere is the motivation.
- ✅ **SleepTown** ([report](../competitive-apps/sleeptown.md)) — From Forest's makers; build virtual buildings by going to bed on time. Using your phone during sleep hours destroys the building under construction.

## Gamified Fitness & Exercise

- ✅ **Zombies, Run!** ([report](../competitive-apps/zombies-run.md)) — Narrative-driven running app: you're "Runner 5" in a post-apocalyptic zombie world, collecting supplies during runs to rebuild Abel Township. 500+ missions across 13+ seasons of professional audio drama.
- ⬜ **Ring Fit Adventure** — Nintendo Switch RPG where every attack is a real exercise (squats, yoga poses, overhead presses); uses a custom Ring-Con and Leg Strap peripheral to detect physical movement.
- ⬜ **Pokemon GO** — Niantic's AR Pokemon collection game tied to real-world walking, egg hatching, and location-based raids. Strongest social/community features of any fitness-adjacent app.
- ⬜ **Walkr** — Cute space-exploration game where your steps fuel a spaceship discovering procedurally revealed planets with unique creatures and lore. Cooperative "Epics" let friends contribute steps together.
- ⬜ **Wokamon** — Step counter that grows cute Tamagotchi-like monsters as you walk. Integrates with Apple Health, Fitbit, and Google Fit.
- ✅ **Darebee Hero's Journey** ([report](../competitive-apps/darebee.md)) — Free web-based 60-day bodyweight RPG program with branching narrative choices that determine each day's workout. No app, no signup, donation-funded.

## Wellness / Mental Health (Gamified)

- ✅ **SuperBetter** ([report](../competitive-apps/superbetter.md)) — Resilience game by game designer Jane McGonigal, backed by 9 peer-reviewed studies. Real-life challenges are reframed as "Bad Guys," supportive friends become "Allies," and small positive actions are "Power-Ups."
- ✅ **Fabulous** ([report](../competitive-apps/fabulous.md)) — Behavioral-science-based habit builder (born from Duke University) using illustrated multi-week "Journeys" to gradually build morning, evening, and lifestyle rituals.

## Meditation

- ⬜ **Calm** — Market-leading meditation app with celebrity-narrated Sleep Stories and a premium nature-photography aesthetic. Deliberately minimal gamification — just streaks.
- ⬜ **Headspace** — Meditation with friendly illustrated mascots and intentionally restrained gamification (streaks, milestone badges, journey maps). 2025 added "mindfulness quests" that drove a 35% increase in weekly active users.

## Sleep

- ⬜ **SleepTown** — (Also listed under Focus Timers.) Build a town by going to bed on time; phone use during sleep hours collapses the in-progress building.

## Language Learning (Gamification Benchmark)

- ✅ **Duolingo** ([report](../competitive-apps/duolingo.md)) — The industry's most sophisticated gamification system, combining streaks, leagues, energy, gems, XP, friend streaks, and an iconic guilt-tripping owl mascot. Widely considered the gold standard and studied by every category.

## Astrology Apps

- ✅ **Co-Star** ([report](../competitive-apps/co-star.md)) — AI-powered astrology with stark black-and-white editorial design and famously blunt push-notification copy. Won a 2024 Webby for Best Visual Design.
- ✅ **Nebula** ([report](sample/nebula.md)) — Astrology and horoscope app with starfield backgrounds, zodiac glyphs, and a deep purple/blue cosmic palette.
- ✅ **The Pattern** ([report](../competitive-apps/the-pattern.md)) — Psychological astrology app with a moody dark UI, teal accents, and personality insights presented as themed cards. Audio insight series add intimacy.
- ✅ **CHANI** ([report](../competitive-apps/chani.md)) — Astrology app by Chani Nicholas with a warm collage-art identity in blush, lavender, and off-white. Inclusive, zine-like, intentionally non-edgy.
- ⬜ **Sanctuary** — Astrology app with live-chat human astrologers, vibrant playful colors, and a friendly messaging-app interface aimed at newcomers.

## Tarot Apps

- ✅ **Golden Thread Tarot** ([report](../competitive-apps/golden-thread-tarot.md)) — Tarot companion app by designer Tina Gong (Labyrinthos) with iconic gold filigree line-art on black. The gold-on-black palette has become a defining reference for modern witchy app design.
- ✅ **Labyrinthos** ([report](../competitive-apps/labyrinthos.md)) — Tina Gong's gamified tarot education platform with lessons, quizzes, and leveling. Minimalist black-and-white line-art with clean UX.
- ✅ **Mystic Mondays** ([report](../competitive-apps/mystic-mondays.md)) — Daily tarot app with vibrant neon-gradient card art by Grace Duong. Holographic edges and cosmic textures lean closer to vaporwave than Rider-Waite.

## Lunar / Moon Phase

- ⬜ **My Moon Phase** — Sleek dark lunar tracker dominated by moon imagery on deep backgrounds. Scientific precision with aesthetic finesse.
- ✅ **Moonly** ([report](../competitive-apps/moonly.md)) — Moon phase tracker with rituals, affirmations, and tarot tied to the lunar cycle. Soft pastel palette with paper-like grain texture.

## Witchy Planners & Digital Grimoires

- ✅ **Writual Planner** ([report](../competitive-apps/writual-planner.md)) — Tarot-integrated planning system with lunar calendar pages and cosmic data tracking. Bridges productivity design and spiritual practice.

## Witchy Games (Cozy / Pastoral)

- ✅ **Wytchwood** ([report](../competitive-apps/wytchwood.md)) — Crafting adventure as an old witch of the woods in a land of gothic fables. Hand-drawn storybook illustration with muted golds, greens, and browns evoking medieval woodcuts.
- ⬜ **Little Witch in the Woods** — Cozy pixel-art game as apprentice witch Ellie gathering ingredients, brewing potions, and helping villagers. Lush greens and soft lighting in fairy-tale storybook quality.
- ⬜ **Wylde Flowers** — Farming/life-sim with a secret witchcraft legacy. Warm rounded 3D characters and inviting environments with autumnal magical touches.
- ⬜ **Magical Delicacy** — Young-witch bakery game with warm European-storybook pixel art and magical touches woven into everyday architecture.
- ✅ **Strange Horticulture** ([report](../competitive-apps/strange-horticulture.md)) — Occult puzzle game running a Victorian plant shop. Hand-drawn muted greens, browns, and purples — 19th-century botanical illustration meets Lovecraftian mystery.
- ✅ **The Cosmic Wheel Sisterhood** ([report](../competitive-apps/cosmic-wheel-sisterhood.md)) — Narrative game where an exiled witch forges her own divination cards to read fates. Manga-influenced pixel art with muted purples, golds, and cosmic backgrounds.
- ⬜ **Potionomics** — Potion-shop management with card-based haggling and Pixar-quality stylized 3D characters in rich saturated colors.
- ⬜ **Moonstone Island** — Creature-collecting farming sim across 100+ floating sky islands. Pastel pixel art with glowing magical elements and soft nature tones.
- ✅ **REKA** ([report](../competitive-apps/reka.md)) — Slavic-folk apprentice game under Baba Jaga: build and customize a chicken-legged hut while practicing witchcraft in autumnal woodlands. Rich amber and forest-green palette.

## Witchy / Occult Games (Dark)

- ✅ **Darkest Dungeon** ([report](../competitive-apps/darkest-dungeon.md)) — Gothic roguelike with Mike-Mignola-inspired ink art designed to look like candlelit manuscript pages. Dark reds, sickly greens, and oppressive shadows.
- ⬜ **Blasphemous** — Brutal action-platformer in a world of twisted Spanish-religious iconography. Pixel art influenced by Goya and Velazquez; dilapidated cathedrals and blood-stained altars.
- ✅ **Inscryption** ([report](../competitive-apps/inscryption.md)) — Genre-defying deckbuilder shifting between a creepy candlelit cabin card game and meta-horror. Folk-horror atmosphere with a shadowy dealer (Leshy) across the table.
- ✅ **Cult of the Lamb** ([report](../competitive-apps/cult-of-the-lamb.md)) — Possessed lamb builds a loyal cult in a land of false prophets. Cutesy-gothic: bright rounded cartoon characters performing dark rituals.
- ⬜ **Slay the Spire** — Genre-defining roguelike deckbuilder with hand-painted dark-fantasy card art. Glowing runes, ancient relics, arcane atmosphere.
- ⬜ **Bayonetta** — Witch action game blending occult symbolism with haute couture and Art Nouveau/Art Deco baroque environments.
- ✅ **Hades** ([report](../competitive-apps/hades.md)) — Roguelike through the Greek Underworld with bold cel-shaded character portraits in vivid reds, golds, and purples. Art-Deco-meets-mythology aesthetic.

## Painterly / Spiritual Games

- ⬜ **Okami** — Shinto sun goddess Amaterasu restoring mythological Japan using the Celestial Brush. Sumi-e and ukiyo-e cel-shading turns the entire world into a living Japanese watercolor scroll.
- ✅ **Spiritfarer** ([report](../competitive-apps/spiritfarer.md)) — Hand-drawn 2D management game about ferrying spirits to the afterlife. Studio-Ghibli-influenced pastel palette with melancholic warmth.
- ✅ **Sayonara Wild Hearts** ([report](sample/sayonara-wild-hearts.md)) — Simogo/Annapurna Interactive's "pop album video game." Neon-on-black rhythm runner with tarot-card chapter breaks, a lone feminine protagonist, and cosmic/lunar imagery woven into every frame. One of the strongest references for Tend's visual register: occult symbolism rendered at AAA aesthetic quality.
